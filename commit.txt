feat(parser): detect circular ${VAR} references and report in lint

Adds DFS-based cycle detection to EnvParser after raw_environ is built.
Each unique cycle emits a warning-level ParseMessage so runenv lint
catches A=${B} / B=${A} patterns. Parsing still completes (circular
vars resolve to their literal ${VAR} form). Five new tests cover direct
cycles, self-references, longer chains, clean files, and parse
resilience.
